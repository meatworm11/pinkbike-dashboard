## Web research

### Search 1: "2020 Evil Offering common issues reliability forum MTBR Reddit"

- **Linkage creaking** is the most commonly reported issue: the lower shock mount bolt (must be greased before torquing) and main pivot are the two reported creak sources. Inspect both on any in-person visit.
- **Loose bolts:** Multiple users report needing periodic bolt checks + Loctite on Evil bikes generally.
- **Service difficulty:** Evil bikes are described as "difficult to deal with when it comes to service — all the models offer slight changes in how their bikes go together." Parts availability and shop familiarity are real concerns for a boutique brand.
- **Warranty concerns:** At least one MTBR thread (Evil and Warranty issues, 2021) noted shops reporting Evil not standing behind warranty claims as reliably as other brands. This predates 2024 layoffs but compounds the concern.

### Search 2: "2020 Evil Offering review enduro trail performance geometry"

- **Geometry:** 477 mm reach in size L, 76° seat tube angle, flip-chip links (LOW and XTRA LOW settings drop BB and slack the head angle). Medium will be meaningfully shorter reach.
- **Performance:** Excels on flow and jump trails; reviewers note the low BB, short wheelbase, and steeper-than-expected head angle (for the era) made it less confidence-inspiring on steep/rough terrain vs. dedicated enduro bikes. This is a trail bike that dips into enduro, not a pure enduro rig.
- **Suspension:** Dave Weagle's Delta System rear linkage. Anti-pedal-bob, good climbing traction. The 140 mm rear / 160 mm fork combo is confirmed from the listing specs.
- Source: [Evil Offering Review | ENDURO Mountainbike Magazine](https://enduro-mtb.com/en/evil-offering-test/), [Vital MTB — 2020 Evil Offering XTR](https://www.vitalmtb.com/product/guide/Bikes,3/Evil/Offering-XTR,29337), [99 Spokes — 2020 Evil Offering GX Eagle](https://99spokes.com/bikes/evil/2020/offering-gx-eagle)
