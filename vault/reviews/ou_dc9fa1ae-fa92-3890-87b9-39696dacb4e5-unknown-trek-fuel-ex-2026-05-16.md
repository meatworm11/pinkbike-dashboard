## Web research

### Search 1: "Trek Fuel EX 8 common issues reliability forum"
- Pivot bearing collapse reported on older models; overtorqued pivot bolts cause creaking and require periodic retightening — worth inspecting before purchase.
- Press-fit bottom bracket creaking develops around 2,000 miles on some builds (sometimes the creak is a bolt, not the BB itself).
- Rear triangle cracking is documented across multiple forum threads — described by some users as "super common." This is a critical inspection point on any used Fuel EX.
- Fox 32 fork on older models (pre-Gen 5) had stiction issues and low oil levels in lower legs.
