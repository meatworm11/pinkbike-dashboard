## Web research

### Search 1: 2023 YT Jeffsy Core 3 common problems reliability
- Drivetrain and frame are reliable: GX drivetrain "crisp and reliable," no
  creaks/rattles; components "faultless" beyond the rear tyre and rotors.
- Known gripes: SRAM G2 R brakes fade on long descents and feel under-gunned for
  sustained gravity; harder dual-compound Maxxis DHR II front tyre feels
  unpredictable (a softer front compound is preferred); V4L suspension squats under
